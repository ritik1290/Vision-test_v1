Astigmatism Clock Dial Test

This test helps detect astigmatism, a condition where the eye focuses light unevenly, causing blurred vision.

  • Sit about 1 meter (3-4 feet) away from the screen.
  • Cover one eye with your hand.
  • Focus on the center of the dial.
  • Observe the lines radiating outwards.

Do all the lines look equally black and sharp?
