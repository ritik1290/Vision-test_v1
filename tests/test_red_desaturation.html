Red Desaturation Test

This test checks for potential optic nerve issues by comparing how bright the color "Red" looks to each eye.

Step 1: Reference Eye

Cover your Left Eye. Look at the red square below with your Right Eye.

Assume this red is "100% Red" or "100 dollars worth of Red".

Step 2: Compare

Now cover your Right Eye and look with your Left Eye.

Does the red look the same, or is it washed out (pinkish/dull)?
