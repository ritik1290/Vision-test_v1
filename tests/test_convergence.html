Convergence Insufficiency (Pencil Push-Up)

This test measures your "Near Point of Convergence" (NPC) - how close you can hold an object before your eyes stop working together.

The Pencil Push-Up Test

1. Hold a pen or pencil at arm's length in front of your nose.

2. Focus on the tip of the pen.

3. Slowly bring the pen closer to your nose, keeping it in focus.

4. Stop when the pen tip splits into TWO images (double vision).
