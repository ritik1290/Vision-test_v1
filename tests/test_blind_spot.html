Blind Spot Mapping

Every eye has a natural blind spot where the optic nerve connects to the retina. This test helps you find yours.

Instructions

1. Close your LEFT eye.

2. Look strictly at the Cross (+) on the left with your Right eye.

3. Move your head closer or further from the screen (approx 10-15 inches) until the Black Dot disappears.
