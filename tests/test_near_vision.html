Near Vision Test

This test checks your ability to read small print at a close distance, which can help detect Presbyopia (age-related farsightedness).

  • Hold your phone or sit about 40 cm (16 inches) from the screen.
  • If you wear reading glasses, put them on.
  • Try to read the paragraphs below, starting from the largest.
J10 (Large Print)
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. Vision health is important for everyone.
J7 (Medium Print)
Regular eye exams are the best way to protect your eyesight. Many eye diseases have no early warning signs.
J5 (Newspaper Print)
Eating a diet rich in fruits and vegetables, particularly dark leafy greens like spinach and kale, is important for keeping your eyes healthy.
J3 (Small Print)
Research has linked smoking to an increased risk of developing age-related macular degeneration, cataract, and optic nerve damage, all of which can lead to blindness.
J1 (Very Small Print)
If you are having trouble reading this text at 40cm, you may be experiencing presbyopia or need a new prescription. Please consult an eye care professional.
