Amsler Grid Test

This test helps detect problems in the macula (the center of the retina), such as Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD).

Instructions:

  1. Wear your reading glasses if you use them.
  2. Hold the screen about 12-15 inches (30-40 cm) away from your face.
  3. Cover one eye.
  4. Focus strictly on the center dot.
  5. While looking at the dot, notice the lines around it.

Focus on the dot for 10 seconds.
