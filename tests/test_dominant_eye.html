Dominant Eye Test

Determine which of your eyes is the "dominant" or sighting eye. This is important for sports, photography, and understanding your vision.

Follow These Steps

Step 1: Create a Triangle

Extend your arms fully in front of you. Bring your hands together to form a small triangular opening between your thumbs and forefingers.

Step 2: Center the Target

With both eyes open, look through the triangle and center the red dot below inside the opening.

Step 3: Close Left Eye

Close your Left Eye. Does the red dot stay centered or move out of view?

Your Result

Your dominant eye is the one that keeps the object centered when the other eye is closed.
