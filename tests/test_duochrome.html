Duochrome Test

This test checks if your vision is properly corrected (not over or under-minused). It uses the principle that red and green light focus at different depths in the eye.

  • Sit about 3 meters (10 feet) away.
  • Cover one eye.
  • Look at the letters on the Red and Green backgrounds.
