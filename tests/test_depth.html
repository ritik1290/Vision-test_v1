Depth Perception (Stereopsis) Check

This test uses the "Finger-Framing" technique to check if your eyes are working together to create depth.

Step 1: Focus on the Red Circle

Sit about 2 feet from the screen. Keep both eyes open and focused on the red circle above.

Step 2: Raise your Finger

Hold your index finger up about 6 inches in front of your nose, directly in line with the red circle.

Step 3: Observe

While keeping your focus on the Red Circle, notice your finger.
